# RO Classic World Assets — Expanded List

Niche regions, sub-detail tiers, and modular components beyond the basic vegetation/architecture/props categories. Use this as an acquisition checklist for populating zones with classic Ragnarok Online flavor.

---

## Less-Common Region Themes

### Umbala (Treetop Jungle Village)
- Treehouse modules (platform + wall + roof, 3 sizes), rope bridges between trunks
- Wooden ladders, climbing pegs
- Tribal totems with feathered tops, war drums, bone fetishes
- Hanging vine curtains, oversized leaves as awnings
- Macaw perches (decorative bird stands)

### Moscovia (Russian)
- Onion-dome chapels (modular: dome + drum + base)
- Birch tree variant (white peeling bark — distinct from pine)
- Painted wooden churches with carved trim
- Matryoshka doll displays, samovar tea sets
- Wooden carved gateways with folk patterns

### Rachel / Veins (Arunafeltz desert temple)
- White marble temple modules (different from Morroc sandstone)
- Goddess pedestal statues, sacred braziers
- Sand-buried ruin segments, eroded statue heads
- Religious altars with sun symbols

### Eclage (Fairy / Elven)
- Giant oversized flowers (mushroom-cap sized — walkable)
- Mushroom houses, tiny doors at tree bases
- Glowing root systems, fairy ring stones
- Silver-thread spider web bridges, hovering moss platforms
- Pollen mote emitters (VFX anchors)

### Brasilis / Dewata / Malaya (Tropical)
- Stilt houses (bahay kubo) with bamboo ladders
- Hanging tropical fruit clusters (bananas, mangoes, coconuts)
- Carnival drums, festival masks, tribal shields
- Outrigger canoes, bamboo torches with palm-frond shades

### Old Glast Heim / Geffenia (Corrupted/Demonic)
- Demon ritual circles (pentagrams, runes — animated emissive)
- Sacrificial altars with chains
- Hellfire braziers (dark red, VFX anchor)
- Skull piles (large heaps, not just bone debris)
- Bleeding wall stains, possessed statues

### Jawaii (Honeymoon Resort)
- Heart-shaped wedding arches, rose petal piles
- Luxury beach huts (cleaner than Morroc tents)
- Romantic dinner tables for two (candles + wine)
- Pink seashell decor, ribbon banners

### Yuno Sage Academy
- Orreries / planet models (animated rotation hookup)
- Lecturing platforms with magical chalkboards
- Floating geometric study shapes (cube, dodecahedron — emissive)
- Crystal study desks, ancient text lecterns

### Pyramid Interior (Morroc Dungeon Sub-Detail)
- Canopic jars (jackal/bird/human-headed lids)
- Ankh wall symbols, scarab inlays
- Treasure heaps (gold piles + jewelry + crowns)
- Spike traps (visual, retracted/extended)
- Mummy upright sarcophagi (open + closed)
- Trap doors / pressure plates

## Modular Building Components (DIY Architecture)

### Walls
- Stone block segments (smooth, rough, mossy, cracked)
- Half-timber Tudor walls (medieval European)
- Brick walls (red, tan, fire-brick)
- Wooden plank walls (vertical, horizontal, lap-joint)
- Wattle-and-daub (rural)
- Marble panels (temple)
- Adobe / stucco (desert)

### Windows (interchangeable in walls)
- Round, arched, gothic-pointed, casement, lattice, leaded glass, stained glass, bay window, dormer

### Doors
- Wooden plank (single + double), iron-bound, stone slab (dungeon), paper-screen sliding (Asian), arched cathedral, secret-passage stone

### Roofs
- Red clay tile (Prontera), slate (Geffen), thatch (Payon/rural), wooden shingle, upturned-eave Asian tile, flat desert roof, dome / cupola modules

### Columns / Pillars
- Doric, Ionic, Corinthian (classical), fluted, twisted (Solomonic), engaged, broken-half, ivy-wrapped

### Roof Accents
- Chimneys (brick, stone, multi-flue), weather vanes (rooster, ship, arrow), finials, gargoyle drainspouts, eave brackets

Region-Specific Decoratives

  Prontera / Izlude (Medieval European)

  - Cathedral interior: pews, altars, pulpits, organs, stained glass windows, candelabras
  - Royal: throne, royal carpet runners, flagpoles with banners, knight statues (full armor poses)
  - Garden: topiaries, hedge mazes, ornamental urns, sundials, stone benches
  - Architectural detail: gargoyles, drainage grates, aqueducts, decorative balustrades

  Geffen (Mage / Gothic)

  - Floor magic circles (glowing runes, modular)
  - Floating crystal clusters (with point lights)
  - Alchemy glassware — bottles, retorts, distillation rigs, beakers
  - Magic tomes / grimoires (open and closed), scroll piles
  - Cauldrons (bubbling, with VFX hookup)
  - Runestones / standing stones (engraved, glowing variants)

  Payon / Amatsu / Louyang / Kunlun (Asian)

  - Pagoda towers (3-5 tier modules)
  - Torii / paper-screen archways
  - Stone garden lanterns (toro), paper lanterns (round + cylindrical)
  - Koi ponds, lily pads, shishi-odoshi (bamboo water clackers)
  - Dol hareubang / dragon / fu dog statues
  - Drying racks (fish, herbs, laundry)
  - Pestle & mortar, tea sets, bamboo water pipes
  - Upturned eaves / Chinese roof corner pieces

  Morroc / Sograt (Desert / Egyptian)

  - Sphinx statues, obelisks, pyramid stone blocks
  - Hieroglyphic wall panels (modular)
  - Sand dunes (decorative meshes), camel posts / hitching rails
  - Tent interiors: carpets, floor cushions, low tables, hookahs
  - Cracked sandstone columns, half-buried ruins

  Comodo / Coast (Carnival + Beach)

  - Carnival striped tents, stage platforms, performer poles
  - Beach umbrellas, deck chairs, driftwood logs
  - Seashells, starfish, sand piles
  - Lighthouses, anchors, rope coils, buoys, fishing nets
  - Crab traps, fish market crates, dinghies / rowboats

  Glast Heim (Ruined Gothic)

  - Broken thrones, smashed stained glass piles

  - Iron maidens, racks, chains, manacles
  - Fallen knight armor (collapsed pose, scattered helms)

  Niflheim (Spooky / Halloween)

  - Jack-o-lanterns (small/large, lit/unlit)

  - Wicker scarecrows (creepy, separate from rural ones)
  - Twisted iron fences with skull finials
  - Soul candles (floating clusters)

  Lutie (Snow / Christmas)

  - Snowmen, snowdrifts (modular piles)

  - Ice crystal formations, frozen pond surfaces
  - Christmas wreaths, garlands, gift boxes, candy canes

  Hugel / Rural Countryside

  - Windmills, watermills (animated blades)

  - Plowed field row meshes, crop plants (wheat, corn)
  - Animal pens — chicken coops, pig styes, troughs
