## Key Decisions

- **No USoundClass** — all bus volumes are manual VolumeMultiplier baked at playback time + live SetVolumeMultiplier on BGM/Ambient components. USoundClass fails in PIE/world transitions.
- **3-layer skill SFX**: SkillImpactSoundMap (per-skill), broadcastToZone enrichment (skillCategory + attackerWeaponType), MAGICAL_DAMAGE_SKILL_IDS exclusion set
- **Variant SFX system**: TArray<FString> + initializer-list lambda + random pick for monster sound variety
- **DO NOT ship original RO audio** — Gravity v. NovaRO $4M judgment (April 2022, settled July 2023). Mirror the architecture but use original/licensed audio.
