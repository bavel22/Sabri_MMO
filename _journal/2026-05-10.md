# 2026-05-10 — Canonical pre-renewal compliance audit (100%)

## Summary

Achieved **byte-for-byte canonical pre-renewal compliance** for all 509 monster templates vs rAthena `db/pre-re/mob_db.yml` master. Built reusable audit infrastructure under `_audits/`. Fixed several long-standing bugs that were making the early game unplayable.

## What Shipped

### Critical bug fixes
- **Monster damage formula** (`ro_damage_formulas.js` `isMonsterAttack` flag) — mobs were getting player-style StatusATK on top of canonical `atk1`/`atk2`, doubling Poring damage from canonical ~8 to broken ~17 vs Novices. Root cause of the "can't beat a Poring without potions" report. `calculateEnemyDamage` now pre-rolls `random(atk1, atk2)`, zeros mob stats (keeps LUK for crit), and passes `isMonsterAttack:true` to skip variance re-roll.
- **Stat point off-by-one** (`ro_exp_tables.js` `getStatPointsForLevelUp`) — was using `floor(newLevel/5)+3` instead of `floor((newLevel-1)/5)+3`. Players gained +19 extra stat points over a 1-99 progression (1244 vs canonical 1225).
- **Refine fees** (`index.js` `REFINE_MATERIALS`) — Wlv3 was 5,000z (5× too expensive), Wlv4 was 20,000z (10× too expensive). Corrected to canonical 1,000z and 2,000z per rAthena `refine_db.txt`.
- **Novice dagger ASPD delay** — was 55, canonical is 50.
- **Skeleton AI mode** — was code 4 (Aggressive), canonical is 17 (Passive + CastSensorIdle). Skeletons no longer aggro players walking by.
- **Drops typo** — "Adventurere's Suit" (3 e's) fixed to "Adventurer's Suit" at 2 spawn sites; was failing item lookup at runtime.
- **Drops display name** — 14 "Ice Piece" references corrected to "Ice Cubic" (canonical display name for id 7066).
