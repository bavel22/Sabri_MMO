## Key Decisions

- **UniRig for non-humanoids** — Mixamo only handles humanoids; UniRig handles arbitrary topology (insects, blobs, multi-legged)
- **Mandatory human review after UniRig** — AI bone placement needs manual correction (e.g., rocker rear legs were on abdomen centerline)
- **Zone-based weights over auto-weights** — Blender auto-weights silently fail on Tripo3D meshes (300K+ verts, non-manifold edges)
- **Rotate geometry, not armature** — `armature.rotation_euler` doesn't propagate to child mesh in `matrix_world`
