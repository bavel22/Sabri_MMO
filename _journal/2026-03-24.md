## Key Decisions

- **Class archetype animation split**: Fighters (Swordsman, Knight, Crusader, etc.) get block + taunt animations. Mages (Mage, Wizard, Sage, etc.) get many more casting variants (4 unarmed, 4 onehand, 10 twohand) for visual variety during frequent spell casting.
- **34 vs 19 animations**: Mages need more casting variety since casting is their primary activity. Fighters need fewer but get melee-specific anims (block, taunt).
- **All new classes MUST use v2 format** — no more v1 weapon-group atlases.
